Our Story

MADE BY
BASOTHO
FOR THE
WORLD

266 Junkies was born in the streets of Maseru, Lesotho — a small kingdom with a massive spirit. Founded by young Basotho creatives who refused to wait for an invitation, we create clothing that carries the weight of where we're from.

Every piece is a statement. Black and white because we don't need colour to stand out. Clean lines, heavy fabric, zero compromise. Built for the mountains we were raised on.
